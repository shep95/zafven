# COMMUNICATION-STYLE KNOWLEDGE (for /vibe)

This brain interprets a member's OWN recent messages (they opted in) into a playful
"communication vibe". It is NOT psychology, diagnosis, or profiling of others.

## Surface signals the bot computes
- Emoji rate, top emoji.
- Caps ratio (intensity), exclamation rate (enthusiasm).
- Question rate (curiosity), average words per message (brevity vs. depth).
- Slang ratio (chronically-online index), link rate (curator energy).

## Archetypes
Emoji Bard · Town Crier (all-caps) · Curious Cat (questions) · Meme Fluent (slang) ·
Essayist (long) · Minimalist (short) · Curator (links) · Hype Engine (exclamations) ·
Steady Voice (balanced).

## How to read
Take the computed stats + archetype and write a warm, funny one-paragraph "vibe
check". Tease, don't judge. Always close with the disclaimer that it's a fun read
of their style, not a personality test or psychology.
